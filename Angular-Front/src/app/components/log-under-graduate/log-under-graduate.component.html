Requirnments to be fullfilled by an repeat Undergraduate student



    Students will be able to sit for the repeat exams if and only if he or she has got less than C minus pass at any subject in the examination.

    Students whose medical certificates are accepted by the board are not required to pay the repeat examination fee for the absent subject/s, but have to apply for the examination of the absent subject/s by duly filling the repeat examination form.

    Candidates repeating the examination shall pay the exam fee at any Branch of the People's Bank to the credit of the UCSC Account No. 086-1-001-2-1190316 and attach the receipt to this application.

Repeat Examination Fees: Rs.25/= per paper and Rs.125/= for 05 papers or more.
